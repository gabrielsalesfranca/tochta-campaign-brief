Part 01 — Foundation

The strategic core

Before any copy or creative direction, these are the strategic truths that govern every decision in this campaign.

The core insight

"RV owners don't buy a mattress. They buy back the trip they already paid for."

The real product

The product is not a mattress — it is the completion of a dream the customer already committed to. The RV cost $80,000. The trip took six months of planning. The factory mattress cost $80 to produce. That gap is the emotional engine of every sale.

Why now

RV sales hit 600,000 units in 2021. Those buyers have now lived with their factory mattresses for 3–5 years. The foam is degrading. The pain is accumulating. The replacement wave is open right now — Tochta needs to own it.

The sleep-joy connection

Research is explicit: poor sleep quality reduces the intensity of positive emotions. The sunrise matters less. The destination lands with less impact. Bad sleep doesn't just make you tired — it steals the feeling of the places you came all this way to see.

The message sequence — non-negotiable

Lead with emotion → bridge with empathy → close with proof. Lead with rational and you lose them. The specs are the proof you bring after you've earned their attention — never the hook.

Hook layer

Emotional. 3–6 words. Speaks to the felt pain. No product, no features. Pure recognition.

"You didn't come this far to sleep this bad."

Empathy bridge

Validates + reframes. 1–2 sentences. Names the real problem and positions Tochta as someone who understands because they lived it.

"Factory RV mattresses are built to a price point. We know — we used to sleep on them too."

Rational proof

3–5 facts that earn the trust the emotional hook created. Factory, certifications, custom builder, trial, warranty.

"Built in our SC factory. Any size, any shape. 100-night trial. 10-year warranty."

Part 02 — Emotional Framework

What Tochta actually sells

Four emotional deliverables — in priority order. Every piece of content maps back to one of these.

Deliverable 01

The completion of the dream

RV ownership is an aspirational act — freedom, family, adventure, escape. The mattress is the last broken piece of that dream. Tochta completes the investment they already made.

Emotional proof: "You didn't spend that much on your rig to wake up sore every morning." Rational proof: 25-year factory, CertiPUR-US, 10-year warranty.

Deliverable 02

Permission to fully enjoy the moment

Sleep science is explicit: poor sleep quality reduces the intensity of positive emotions. Tochta doesn't sell rest — it sells the ability to feel the trip you're on.

Emotional proof: "Wake up ready to actually be where you are." Rational proof: Gel cooling, progressive support layers, quick-recovery foam.

Deliverable 03

Peace between partners

The most cited pain across all personas. One runs hot, one needs firmer, one moves all night. The Divini dual-zone is the only product in the niche that resolves this directly.

Emotional proof: "Finally — a mattress that doesn't make you negotiate your sleep." Rational proof: 9 firmness levels per side, 81 combinations, patent-pending.

Deliverable 04

The end of compromise

Every other brand asks RV owners to adapt to what's available. Tochta is the first to ask: "Tell us what you need and we'll build it." A fundamentally different emotional offer.

Emotional proof: "Your RV is custom. Your mattress should be too." Rational proof: Custom shape builder, instant quote, any dimension.
